Signin Fields

Username

A username must have at least 3 and at most 32 characters.

Characters may be any letter, number, or any of the following: !, @, #, $, %, ^, &, *, (, ), -, _, +, =, or a period or comma

Usernames are not case-sensitive (for example, STUART is the same as stuart).

Password

For your protection, a password must meet the following requirements:

  • A password must contain at least 8 characters and no more than 32 characters.
  • At least one character must be lowercase.
  • At least one character must be uppercase.
  • At least one character must be a number.
  • At least one character must be one of the following: !, @, $, ^, *, (, ), -, _, +, =, :, a period, or a comma. Spaces are not allowed.
  • Passwords are case-sensitive (for example, @morNing2 is not the same as @Morning2).

Email Address

This must be a valid email address that you own. We use the email address to verify that you are the account holder and to help you recover your account in case you forget your password.
